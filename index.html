About Dear Story

Dear Story is a professional music studio that helps non-musicians create and record their own songs like real artists. With the global popularity of K-Pop, we provide international visitors to Korea with the opportunity to experience the K-Pop production process firsthand and dive deeper into Korean culture. We offer customized K-Pop song creation and recording services, allowing our guests to create special memories during their Korean journey and leave with a one-of-a-kind song that belongs only to them.
